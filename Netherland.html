Dresses of Netherlands

The Netherlands has a rich history of traditional clothing, with different costumes found in different regions of the country
1. The Vollendame costume consisting of the black skirt, stripped apron, shawl and a lace hat along with wooden shoes (Klompen) are what most people believe to be the traditional dress.
2. However, the Netherlands does not have a national traditional costume.
3. In the Netherlands, there are 14 provinces and each of them has its own style of national attire, its own traditional accessories, etc. You can find more information about Dutch clothing in the Dutch clothing category on Wikipedia.
